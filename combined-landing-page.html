About the Bureau

WhoWe Are

The Japan India Business Bureau (JIBB) is a non-profit organization dedicated to strengthening economic, industrial, and cultural ties between Japan and India. Positioned as a strategic bridge, we enable collaboration by connecting businesses, institutions, and government stakeholders across both ecosystems.

JP

Tokyo, Japan

Headquarters

Central hub for policy alignment, institutional outreach, and ministerial-level engagement across the Japanese business ecosystem.

IN

Noida, India

Operations Center

Regional command center for industrial implementation, site management, and business scaling within the Indian territory.

Our Vision
visibility

A Collaborative Future as Global Leaders

To create a collaborative future where Japan and India stand together as global leaders in technology, industry, and sustainable growth—two nations united in purpose, driving innovation that shapes the world.

Our Mission

Driving Meaningful Partnerships & Progress

To drive meaningful partnerships and industrial progress by providing expert market entry support, fostering cross-border collaboration, and delivering world-class innovation incubation for businesses and governments across both nations.
