Japan India Business Bureau (JIBB)
Empowering Innovation. Building Lasting Partnerships.

About Us
Welcome to the Japan India Business Bureau (JIBB). We serve as a strategic bridge connecting businesses, governments, and startups across Japan and India. Our goal is to empower innovation and facilitate cross-border collaborations that drive mutual growth.
Navigating a new market involves unique cultural and regulatory landscapes. We understand these nuances. By bridging the gap between Japanese and Indian industries, we help you overcome barriers and connect seamlessly. Whether you want to expand your corporate footprint, find reliable manufacturing partners, or scale a new technology, we guide you through every step of the journey.
Vision and Mission
Our Vision
To create a collaborative future where Japan and India stand together as global leaders in technology, industry, and sustainable growth.
Our Mission
To drive meaningful partnerships and industrial progress by providing expert market entry support, fostering cross-border collaboration, and delivering world-class innovation incubation for businesses and governments.
Our Services
We offer a comprehensive suite of services to ensure your cross-border success. Our team connects you with the right stakeholders and provides the insights you need to make informed, strategic decisions.
Market Entry Support
Expanding into new territory requires clear guidance. We help Japanese companies enter the Indian market and assist Indian businesses expanding into Japan. We provide step-by-step entry strategies, localization support, and deep regulatory insights to ensure a smooth transition.
Partnership Facilitation
Finding the right partner is critical for long-term success. We help you identify reliable customers, joint venture candidates, and manufacturing partners. If you need a specific technology partner in Japan or a vast production network in India, we connect you with trusted industry leaders.
Co-Innovation Collaboration
We actively facilitate joint projects between Indian and Japanese firms. By bringing diverse minds together, we help you share resources, reduce development costs, and create groundbreaking products for the global market.
Diaspora Networking
We organize exclusive networking events to connect the Indian diaspora with vibrant Japanese business opportunities. These clubs foster mutual understanding and create strong, personal bonds that translate into professional success.
The JIBB Innovation Hub
At the heart of our mission is the JIBB Innovation Hub. Located in both Tokyo and Noida, this dedicated space is where raw ideas become market-ready solutions.
What We Offer
* State-of-the-Art Laboratories: We provide advanced research and development facilities. Businesses and researchers can access high-tech equipment to test, refine, and perfect their products, particularly in the electronics, EV, and pharma sectors.
* Startup Incubation: We give emerging startups the foundation they need to thrive. Our incubation program offers workspace, strategic mentorship, and direct access to investors from both nations.
* Expert Ecosystem: When you work in the Innovation Hub, you surround yourself with industry experts, government officials, and ambitious entrepreneurs. This environment naturally breeds creativity and accelerates technology adoption.
