About Us -

The Japan India Business Bureau (JIBB) is a non-profit organization dedicated to strengthening economic, industrial, and cultural ties between Japan and India. Positioned as a strategic bridge between the two nations, JIBB enables meaningful collaboration by connecting stakeholders, businesses, institutions, and government stakeholders across both ecosystems.
With its headquarters in Tokyo, Japan, and an operational presence in Noida, India, JIBB actively facilitates cross-border partnerships, market entry, and long-term industrial cooperation. The organization supports companies in navigating regulatory landscapes, identifying opportunities, and building sustainable business relationships in both countries. JIBB focuses on creating structured platforms for engagement, knowledge exchange, and industry development.
JIBB works across key sectors including Electronics & Semiconductors, Automotive & Electric Vehicles, Renewable Energy, Pharmaceuticals, Infrastructure, Chemicals, Advanced Manufacturing, and other Emerging Areas that are central to the future of India-Japan collaboration.
Through close coordination with government bodies, industry associations, and private enterprises, JIBB aims to:
* Promote trade and investment opportunities 
* Facilitate strategic partnerships and joint ventures 
* Support companies in market expansion and localization 
* Encourage innovation-led industrial growth
* Break the cultural differences between two nations
* Bud innovations from the scratch and then facilitate in global expansions 
At its core, JIBB is committed to building long-term, trust-based partnerships that contribute to sustainable economic growth and shared prosperity between Japan and India.


Who We Are
JIBB is a cross-border industry facilitation platform that brings together businesses, institutions, and government stakeholders from Japan and India. We are not just a network, but an enabler of meaningful partnerships built on trust, transparency, and long-term vision.
With a strong presence in both Tokyo and Noida, we operate at the intersection of two dynamic economies, helping organizations connect, collaborate, and grow beyond geographical and cultural boundaries. Our strength lies in understanding both ecosystems deeply and translating that understanding into actionable opportunities for our stakeholders.

What We Do
JIBB acts as a catalyst for India-Japan collaboration by creating structured pathways for businesses to engage, expand, and succeed.
We facilitate market entry and expansion by helping companies navigate regulatory frameworks, identify the right partners, and localize their operations effectively. Through curated networking platforms, industry events, and strategic introductions, we enable high-value collaborations and joint ventures.
We also drive knowledge exchange by bringing together industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators to share insights and explore emerging opportunities. From early-stage ideas to large-scale industrial partnerships, JIBB supports the entire journey-enabling innovation, fostering growth, and accelerating global expansion.

What We Believe
We believe that the future of India-Japan collaboration lies in long-term partnerships built on mutual trust and shared value.
We believe in bridging not just businesses, but cultures-because true collaboration goes beyond transactions. By fostering deeper understanding and alignment, we help organizations work together more effectively.
We believe in innovation as a driver of growth, supporting ideas from their inception and enabling them to scale globally.
Above all, we believe in creating impact-where collaboration leads to sustainable development, stronger industries, and a shared economic future for both nations.
